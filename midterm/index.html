About us

Welcome to TB Carnival! Here you can play all kinds of games and have fun. Established in 1899, TB Carnival has moved from state to state and spread the joy of fun throughout all North America! For the small price of $5, you can enter and have access to our many games, including Whac-a-Mole, Cornhole, and Shell game. As of right now, Shell Game is only available, however soon, we will have all our games up and running.

As previously stated, we have a plethora of games available at TB Carnival. The games we have consistenly had throughout our establishment are the infamous Shell game, Cornhole, Rock-Paper-Scissors, and Whac-a-Mole. If won, you win a set amount of points and can cash it in for a prize!

The prizes we have here are stuffed animals. Here we have Tony the Rabbit, Frank the Bear, Tobey the Tiger, and Earl the Elephant! Play a handful of games and you too can take one of these prizes home. Good luck!
